A long time ago in a galaxy far, far away...

Welcome to Star Wars: The RPG, the unofficial Star Wars battle game.

The game works by choosing your character and then selecting a foe to fight with one on one.

Player characters have a base attack that increases by its base with every attack. Enemy Defenders have a counter attack that remains unchanged throughout your battle.

The key to victory is picking your foe wisely in order to build up your attack power. To defeat all your enemies you will have to survive to the very end.

May the force be with you.
